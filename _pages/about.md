Shiwei Liu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic and Information Engineering at Tongji University.
He received the Ph.D. degree in 2024 from the Institute of Microelectronics, Fudan University, under the supervision of [Prof. Qi Liu](https://icmne.fudan.edu.cn/2d/2a/c48925a732458/page.htm) and [Prof. Chixiao Chen](https://icmne.fudan.edu.cn/2c/a5/c48925a732325/page.htm), selected for the CCF Incentive Program for Doctoral Dissertations in Integrated Circuit Design (CCF集成电路设计专业委员会博士学位论文激励计划).
Dr.Liu's research interests include AI chip, in-memory computing and 2.5D/3D chip.
He has published over 20 papers in top-tier conferences and journals in the field of integrated circuits and computer architecture, including ISSCC, CICC, DAC, ICCAD, TCAS-I, etc.

Research Interests
======

* Architecture–algorithm co-design for efficient AI with model compression.
* Compute-in-memory circuit and architecture design for AI acceleration.
* Scalable 2.5D/3D chiplet architecture and chip design.
