I was honored to serve as a Session Co-Chair for **Quantization, Approximation, and Compression for ML Hardware I** at the IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems 2026 in Shanghai.

IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems (ISCAS) is the flagship conference of the IEEE Circuits and Systems Society and one of the most prestigious international conferences in the field of circuits, systems, and integrated computing technologies.

The session focuses on efficient machine learning hardware techniques that reduce computation, memory, and energy consumption through model compression, low-precision quantization, and approximate computing methods.

**The session will be held from 10:30-12:00 on Tuesday, May 26, 2026 (UTC+8), at the Shanghai International Convention Center, Room 5D.**
Look forward to discussing and exchanging ideas with researchers in the field.
